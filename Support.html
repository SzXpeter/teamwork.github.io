Ana

One of the founding members of Overwatch, Ana uses her skills and expertise to defend her home and the people she cares for.

Abilities

Biotic Rifle

Long-range rifle that heals allies and damages enemies.

Sleep Dart

Fires a dart that puts an enemy to sleep.

Biotic Grenade

Throws a grenade that heals and increases healing on allies, while damaging and preventing healing on enemies.

Nano Boost

Increases an ally's damage, while reducing damage taken.

Baptiste

An elite combat medic and ex-Talon operative, Baptiste now uses his skills to help those whose lives have been impacted by war.

Abilities

Biotic Launcher

Three-round burst gun.

Regenerativ Burst

Activate to heal yourself and nearby allies instantly, with additional healing over time. Instant heal is doubled for targets less than half health.

Immortality Field

Toss a device that prevents allies from dying. The device can be destroyed.

Amplification Matrix

Project a matrix that doubles the damage and healing effects of allied projectiles.

Exo Boots

Hold crouch to jump higher.

Brigitte

No longer sitting on the sidelines, Brigitte Lindholm has taken up arms to defend those in need of protection.

Abilities

Rocket Flail

Melee weapon with extended range.

Repair Pack

Heals an ally for a short duration.

Whip Shot

Launch your flail forward to knock an enemy away from you.

Barrier Shield

Hold to deploy a frontal energy barrier.

Shield Bash

Available when Barrier Shield is deployed. Dash forward to knock back an enemy.

Rally

Move faster and provide extra health to nearby allies.

Inspire

Dealing damage to enemies heals nearby allies.

Kiriko

Lúcio

Lúcio is an international celebrity who inspires social change through his music and activism.

Mercy

A guardian angel to those who come under her care, Dr. Angela Ziegler is a peerless healer, a brilliant scientist, and a staunch advocate for peace.

Moira

Equal parts brilliant and controversial, scientist Moira O'Deorain is on the cutting edge of genetic engineering, searching for a way to rewrite the fundamental building blocks of life.

Zenyatta

Zenyatta is an omnic monk who wanders the world in search of spiritual enlightenment. It is said that those who cross his path are never the same again.
