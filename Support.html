Ana

One of the founding members of Overwatch, Ana uses her skills and expertise to defend her home and the people she cares for.

Abilities

Biotic Rifle

Long-range rifle that heals allies and damages enemies.

Sleep Dart

Fires a dart that puts an enemy to sleep.

Biotic Grenade

Throws a grenade that heals and increases healing on allies, while damaging and preventing healing on enemies.

Nano Boost

Increases an ally's damage, while reducing damage taken.

Baptiste

An elite combat medic and ex-Talon operative, Baptiste now uses his skills to help those whose lives have been impacted by war.

Abilities

Biotic Launcher

Three-round burst gun.

Regenerativ Burst

Activate to heal yourself and nearby allies instantly, with additional healing over time. Instant heal is doubled for targets less than half health.

Immortality Field

Toss a device that prevents allies from dying. The device can be destroyed.

Amplification Matrix

Project a matrix that doubles the damage and healing effects of allied projectiles.

Exo Boots

Hold crouch to jump higher.
